My Goals

  1. Why do I want to learn this skill?

    I want to learn this skill to obtain a better job. One more suited for someone with adhd. One that will allow me the Freedom to work from anywhere so I will never be tied to one location again.

  2. What will I achieve if I learn this skill?

    With this skill, I will not only be able to obtain the ideal job, I will be able to help Linda with her future projects and pass down knowledge to my son who will likely see similar challenges in his life.

  3. How will this skill change my life and my career?

    I will finally be free to work from anywhere. I can travel again without the need for approval or pto.

  4. How will learning this skill impact the lives of my family, friends and coworkers?

    My friends will likely be inspired by my late career shift. They may even be motivated to pursue a new path themselves. I will also be more respected among my siblings.

  5. How will I feel if I never accomplish this?

    I can't afford to miss this opportunity. I need to gain back control over my own life again. To escape the depressing existence I am living with my current job.

  6. What would my life look like if I manage to accomplish this?

    I would be so at peace. I could finally see the world without worrying that I'm not earning money. I could wake up when I want, kiss my love and enjoy sunshine streaming in through the windows of my latest venture location. My job would be the best type of challenge for me without me having to sacrifice my love for traveling and seeing new things or simply enjoying the company of people I am visiting.
